- Hero style: tiny pixel human with white square head and red shirt

## Character Spec
- Small pixel silhouette, high readability
- White square head
- Red shirt
- Dark legs
- White arm accent

## UI Rules
- Keep overlays soft and readable over teal background
- Preserve playful and bright contrast
- Keep HUD compact with strong text legibility
